Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up call forwarding?

To set up call forwarding:

  1. Go to your phone settings
  2. Select "Call Forwarding" or "Call Settings"
  3. Enter the forwarding number: +91 1800-ROADAI-24
  4. Enable call forwarding
  5. Test by calling your business number

Need detailed instructions? Check our setup guide or contact support.

Can I customize the AI's responses?

Yes! You can fully customize your AI agent:

  • Greeting messages
  • Voice type and speed
  • Languages supported
  • Call handling behavior

Go to AI Settings to make changes.

How does billing work for the free trial?

Your free trial includes 30 minutes of calling time with no credit card required. After the trial:

  • You can upgrade to a paid plan to continue
  • Your AI agent will pause if you don't upgrade
  • All your data and settings are saved
  • You can upgrade anytime to reactivate

What languages are supported?

RoadAI Assistant supports 10+ Indian languages:

  • English and Hindi (most popular)
  • Tamil, Telugu, Bengali
  • Marathi, Gujarati
  • Kannada, Malayalam
  • Punjabi and more

The AI can auto-detect the customer's language and switch automatically.

Can I transfer calls to human agents?

Yes! RoadAI supports intelligent call transfer:

  • Automatic transfer for emergencies
  • Manual transfer when customer requests
  • Transfer based on complexity
  • Configurable transfer rules

Set up transfer rules in AI Settings.

How accurate is the AI?

Our AI uses advanced speech recognition and natural language processing:

  • 95%+ accuracy in understanding requests
  • Works well in noisy environments
  • Handles accents and dialects
  • Continuously learning and improving

Test it yourself with a call to see the quality!
